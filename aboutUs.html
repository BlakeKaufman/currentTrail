Our Values at Currentrail

01
Be Transparent Currentrail places user privacy and experience as the utmost priority. Your information will be kept entirely secure and only utilized in your best interest. Please look at our privacy policy for more information or contact our team for any questions.
02
Be Reliable The founding team at Currentrail was strategically selected to best relate to our users. Our three founders are all college students who face the same challenges as you, and we are thrilled to provide a solution.
03
Be Relentless Our team is driven to solve the problem at hand effectively and will continue to enhance the platform to meet the needs of our users. You are our priority, so we value your feedback!
04
Be Understanding Currentrail is designed to supplement and ease the internship recruitment and career planning process, but it by no means, takes you to the finish line. By providing us with more information about yourself and your goals, we can offer an excellent set of tools to propel you forward - but only you can cross the goal line.
